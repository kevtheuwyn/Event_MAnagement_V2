Genre of Music Events in Malaysia!

Music can be divided into genres in varying ways, such as popular music and art music, or religious music and secular music. The artistic nature of music means that these classifications are often subjective and controversial, and some genres may overlap. In Eventos, we most focus on Jazz Music, Pop Music and Rock Music!
There will have more different genres of music in the future! Please stay tuned for future updates!
